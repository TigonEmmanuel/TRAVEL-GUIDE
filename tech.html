FutureTech 2025 Conference

March 15, 2025 · Accra International Conference Centre, Ghana

Join us at FutureTech 2025 — a premier tech event bringing together developers, innovators, and industry leaders to explore the future of technology. Engage in expert-led sessions, hands-on workshops, and valuable networking opportunities.

Speakers

Dr. Ama Boateng — AI Research Scientist

Dr. Ama Boateng is an internationally recognized AI researcher and professor at the University of Ghana, with over 15 years of experience in machine learning and data science. She has contributed to groundbreaking research in ethical AI and has been a keynote speaker at tech summits across Africa and Europe. At FutureTech 2025, she will present “The Future of Ethical AI in Africa,” exploring how AI can be developed responsibly to solve real-world problems in healthcare, education, and agriculture.

Kwame Mensah — Chief Technology Officer, FinTech Global

Kwame Mensah is a visionary technology leader who helped scale FinTech Global into one of the fastest-growing digital banking platforms in West Africa. With over two decades of experience in software architecture and financial systems, he specializes in building robust and inclusive digital products. His session, “Scaling FinTech in Emerging Markets,” will provide actionable insights for startups and developers working in the finance sector.

Nana Ekua Asare — Cybersecurity Consultant & Digital Safety Advocate

Nana Ekua Asare is a respected cybersecurity consultant with clients across Africa, Europe, and North America. A strong advocate for digital literacy and safety, she regularly trains businesses and schools on how to protect sensitive data and avoid cyber threats. Her talk, “Hacking the Hackers: Cybersecurity in the Age of AI,” will feature real-life case studies and tips on building secure systems in a rapidly evolving digital world.
