Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.

Nutritional Information

This dish provides a good balance of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats. It’s a hearty meal that supports energy levels and muscle maintenance.
